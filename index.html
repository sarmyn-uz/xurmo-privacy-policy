Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Xurmo values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information when you use the Xurmo mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from regular users (customers)

We collect and store the following personal information from regular users:

  • First name and last name
  • Phone number
  • Email address

1.2 Information from sellers

To ensure platform security and trust, we collect additional information from sellers, including:

  • Passport or identity document information
  • Selfie photographs for identity verification

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining application functionality
  • User and seller identification and verification
  • Improving service quality and user experience
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud
  • Communicating important updates and notifications

3. Permissions Required by the App

Xurmo requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • Notification access – to send important updates and alerts
  • Camera access – to capture photos for verification and uploads
  • Gallery access – to select and upload images
  • Location (GPS) access – to enable map and location-based services

4. Third-Party Services

Xurmo uses trusted third-party services to operate efficiently:

  • Cloudflare – for security, performance, and protection against attacks
  • Yandex Maps – for map display and location-based features
These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide our services.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of their data
  • Delete their account

7. Children's Privacy

Xurmo is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Maxfiylik Siyosati

Oxirgi yangilanish: Yanvar 2026

Xurmo foydalanuvchilarning shaxsiy ma’lumotlari maxfiyligini hurmat qiladi. Ushbu Maxfiylik Siyosati Xurmo mobil ilovasidan foydalanish jarayonida ma’lumotlar qanday yig‘ilishi, ishlatilishi, saqlanishi va himoyalanishini tushuntiradi.

1. Qanday ma’lumotlarni yig‘amiz

1.1 Oddiy foydalanuvchilar (xaridorlar)

Ilovadan foydalanayotgan oddiy foydalanuvchilardan quyidagi ma’lumotlar yig‘iladi va saqlanadi:

  • Ism va familiya
  • Telefon raqam
  • Elektron pochta manzili

1.2 Sotuvchilar

Platforma xavfsizligini ta’minlash maqsadida sotuvchilardan qo‘shimcha ma’lumotlar talab qilinadi:

  • Pasport yoki shaxsni tasdiqlovchi hujjat ma’lumotlari
  • Shaxsni tasdiqlash uchun selfi rasmlar

2. Ma’lumotlardan qanday foydalanamiz

  • Ilova funksiyalarini ta’minlash
  • Foydalanuvchi va sotuvchilarni aniqlash hamda tasdiqlash
  • Xizmat sifatini yaxshilash
  • Xavfsizlikni ta’minlash va firibgarlikning oldini olish
  • Muhim bildirishnomalar va xabarlarni yuborish

3. Ilova so‘raydigan ruxsatlar

  • Bildirishnomalar – muhim xabarlarni yuborish uchun
  • Kamera – rasmga olish va hujjatlarni yuklash uchun
  • Galereya – rasmlarni tanlash va yuklash uchun
  • Joylashuv (GPS) – xarita va joylashuv xizmatlari uchun

4. Uchinchi tomon xizmatlari

  • Cloudflare – xavfsizlik va server himoyasi uchun
  • Yandex Xaritalar – xarita va joylashuv funksiyalari uchun

Ushbu xizmatlar o‘zlarining maxfiylik siyosatlariga muvofiq ma’lumotlarni qayta ishlashi mumkin.

5. Ma’lumotlarni saqlash va himoyalash

Biz foydalanuvchi ma’lumotlarini himoyalash uchun zarur texnik va tashkiliy choralarni ko‘ramiz. Ma’lumotlar faqat xizmat ko‘rsatish uchun zarur bo‘lgan muddat davomida saqlanadi.

6. Foydalanuvchi huquqlari

  • Shaxsiy ma’lumotlarini ko‘rish
  • Ma’lumotlarni o‘zgartirish yoki o‘chirishni so‘rash
  • Akkauntni o‘chirish

7. Bolalar maxfiyligi

Xurmo ilovasi 13 yoshdan kichik bolalar uchun mo‘ljallanmagan va ularning ma’lumotlarini ataylab yig‘maydi.

8. Siyosatdagi o‘zgarishlar

Ushbu Maxfiylik Siyosati vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilanishi mumkin. O‘zgarishlar ushbu sahifada e’lon qilinadi.
